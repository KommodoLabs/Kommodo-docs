# Glossary

## Asset
While a digital asset can take many forms, the Kommodo Protocol supports ERC20 token pairs, and represents a lender position in the form of an NFT (ERC-721).

## Collateral
Digital asset pledged as security for repayment of a loan, to be forfeited in the event of a default.

## Concentrated Liquidity
Liquidity that is allocated within a determined price range.

## ERC20
ERC20 tokens are fungible tokens on Ethereum. Kommodo supports all standard ERC20 implementations.

## Factory
A smart contract that deploys a unique smart contract for any ERC20/ERC20 trading pair.

## Liquidity Provider / "LP"
A liquidity provider is someone who deposits ERC20 tokens into a given liquidity pool. Liquidity providers take on price risk and are compensated with trading fees and lending fees.

## Liquidity
Digital assets that are stored in a Uniswap pool contract, and are able to be traded against by traders and borrowed by borrowers.

## Loan
Digital asset that is borrowed, that is expected to be paid back with interest.

## Pool
A contract deployed by the factory that pairs two ERC20 assets. Different pools may have different fees despite containing the same token pair. 

## Position
An instance of liquidity defined by upper and lower tick. And the amount of liquidity contained therein.

## Range
Any interval between two ticks of any distance.

## Slippage
The total difference between the expected price at the time of submitting a transaction and the actual execution price, which may include price impact and other market movements that occur before the transaction is mined.

## Spacing
The space between two nearest ticks.

## Spot Price
The current price of a token relative to another within a given pair.

## Tick
The boundaries between discrete areas in price space.
